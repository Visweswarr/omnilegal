api.parliament.uk is the application programming interface for some of the procedural data for the UK parliament Data is licensed under the Open Parliament Licence - see https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/copyright-parliament/open-parliament-licence/ id.parliament.uk is the namespace for resources belonging to the Parliament of the United Kingdom (see https://id.parliament.uk/) The following endpoints are also available: - An OData endpoint: https://api.parliament.uk/odata/ - A public SPARQL endpoint: https://api.parliament.uk/sparql/ - A parameterised query endpoint: https://api.parliament.uk/query/ Cookie policy: https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/privacy/ Accessibility statement: https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/accessibility/ Privacy notice: https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/data-protection/data-protection-and-privacy-policy/
